Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an ID card?
To create an ID card, simply open the app, choose a template, upload a photo, fill in the required information, and customize the design as needed. Once you're satisfied, you can save or export your ID card.
Can I use my own custom design?
Yes, our app allows you to create custom designs. You can start with a blank template and add your own elements, or modify existing templates to suit your needs.
What file formats can I export my ID card in?
You can export your ID cards in various formats, including PNG, JPEG, and PDF. This allows for easy sharing and printing of your created ID cards.
