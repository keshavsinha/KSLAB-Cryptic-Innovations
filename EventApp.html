Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an event?
To create an event, simply open the app, choose 'Create Event', fill in the required information, and customize as needed. Once you're satisfied, you can publish your event.
What's the difference between Free and Paid versions?
The Free version offers basic event management features, while the Paid version includes advanced analytics, custom branding, enhanced QR code features, and more. Check our Pricing section for a detailed comparison.
