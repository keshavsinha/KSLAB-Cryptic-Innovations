Profile Summary

Dr. Keshav Sinha is an Assistant Professor with a focus on Cryptography and Network Security, specializing in creating secure environments for multimedia transmission. His extensive research and numerous publications span conferences and journals in Soft Computing, Humanities, and cyber policy. Dr. Sinha is dedicated to enhancing flexibility and innovation within the computer science community.

Prior to his current role at UPES, Dr. Sinha served as an Assistant Professor at Sarala Birla University, and held positions as a Faculty Associate and Guest Faculty at Usha Martin University. He also gained valuable experience as a Project Assistant Level-II at CSIR-CIMFR and ECL in Dhanbad and Kajora Area.

Dr. Sinha boasts a significant research record with 3 SCIE journal papers, 10 Scopus-indexed book chapters, and 9 Scopus conference papers. His work covers data security, security protocols, optimization, and cyber policies. He is a respected reviewer for IGI Global, Journal of Intelligent and Fuzzy Systems (JIFS), and other notable journals. Dr. Sinha has also guided M.Tech and Ph.D. scholars, contributing to their academic and research growth. His commitment to research and mentoring exemplifies his dedication to advancing the field and inspiring his students to excel.
